Your custom LOVE marker

Step 1: Generate the Pattern File

  1. Open the AR.js Marker Training Tool
  2. Click "Upload" and select your marker image
  3. Use this image: markers/love-marker.svg (or take a screenshot of it)
  4. Click "Download Marker" to get the .patt file
  5. Save it as markers/love.patt in your project

Step 2: Download the Marker Image

Right-click the marker above and "Save Image As" to print it or display on screen.

Or download: love-marker.svg

Step 3: Update ar.html

Once you have the .patt file, the AR will automatically use your custom LOVE marker!
